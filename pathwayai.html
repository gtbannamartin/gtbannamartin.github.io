Not a pilot. Not a prototype. PathwayAI is production-deployed agentic AI — the same autonomous architecture powering every DJMP service, running live in Chicagoland right now. The same system Google.org has been asked to fund at $1.5M for national scale.

The Solution

Three Autonomous Agents.
Running 24 Hours a Day.

PathwayAI deploys three purpose-built AI agents that work continuously — no human required — to connect every community member to the resources they need.

🗺️
Agent 01
Skills & Opportunity Mapping

Monitors BLS Occupational Outlook and O*NET in real time — mapping exactly what jobs are available, what they pay, and what skills unlock them for each community member.

🔍
Agent 02
Training Resource Discovery

Autonomously searches WIOA, CareerOneStop, apprenticeship.gov, and College Scorecard — returning a ranked, personalized list of publicly funded training opportunities available right now.

🚀
Agent 03
Pathway Progression & Career Bridge

Builds a complete, milestone-based career roadmap for each resident — delivered via web, mobile, or SMS. No smartphone required. No one left out.
