LinkedIn — Mission
Every year, billions in publicly funded workforce training never reaches the people it was built for. That is not a funding failure. It is an infrastructure failure. DJMP Institute builds autonomous AI infrastructure that connects marginalized urban residents directly to the publicly funded education and training resources that are their most accessible pathway to economic opportunity. Our flagship platform, PathwayAI, deploys three autonomous AI agents powered by Google Gemini — continuously monitoring ten federal databases and delivering personalized career pathways via web, mobile, and SMS so no resident is left out. If that mission matters to you — support it. Share it. Build with us. djmpinstitute.org

LinkedIn — PathwayAI Feature
Over 68 million Americans lack the digital literacy to navigate the government workforce training systems designed to help them. PathwayAI changes that. Three autonomous AI agents powered by Google Gemini continuously monitor ten publicly available federal databases — delivering personalized, step-by-step career pathways to marginalized residents via web, mobile, and SMS. Projected outcomes over 36 months: → 40% increase in WIOA training enrollment → 500K+ residents reached nationally → Deployment in 25+ cities We are seeking partners, funders, and government agencies ready to build this infrastructure together. Learn more at djmpinstitute.org
