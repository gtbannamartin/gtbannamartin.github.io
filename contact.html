Our Historic Home

DJMP Institute is proud to be headquartered in the historic Overton Hygienic Building — one of the most significant landmarks of Chicago's Black business heritage, built in 1922 by entrepreneur and civic leader Anthony Overton on the city's South Side. Our presence in this building reflects our commitment to the communities and the legacy of Black entrepreneurship and civic excellence that has always defined this neighborhood.
