Where This Began · 2017
“They didn’t ask for a classroom assignment. They mapped every shooting in Chicago and built a safer route home.”
Martin Pieters · STEM Director, Rainbow PUSH Coalition · Saturday Academy 2017

DJMP students mapped every shooting in Chicago from May–June 2017, built a heat map giving students a safer route home, and unveiled the Stay Safe App at Rainbow PUSH’s 46th Annual Convention. Covered by ABC7 and the Chicago Sun-Times. Recognized by the Cook County Board of Commissioners. That is who our students are.

The architects of tomorrow will come from the communities that need them most — because we refused to let them be anything less.
